Snake

Use the arrow keys to move the snake around the screen and collect the red food. Each time you eat, your score will increase and your snake will get bigger. Avoid hitting your tail and get as much food as you can. Please calibrate eyetracking for this game.

Rubick's Race
Slide the squares on the right to match the pattern on the left. The number of moves you take to complete the puzzle is your score. Unlike the other games, try to keep your score as low as possible by completing the game in as few moves as possible. Please calibrate eyetracking for this game.

Dots And Boxes
Place horizontal or vertical lines to create boxes. When you complete the fourth side of a box you then get a bonus turn. When all the boxes have been completed the winner is the player who has completed the most boxes. The secondary player of this game is an AI. Please ensure your game is set up to have 1 Human Player and 1 AI player. Please calibrate eyetracking for this game.
